About

Italian restaurant was opened by award winning Chef Luigi Mario to offer residents and visitors of southern Sometown with the authentic Italian cuisine that the Mario name has become synonymous with after the success of the Mario restaurant in Las Vegas. The restaurant now has a loyal following of its own, offering traditional Roman recipes passed down from Chef Luigi's family, as well as inventive Northern and Southern Italian preparations inspired by Chef Luigi's culinary education and travels.
